Christopher's Classroom

Welcome to Christopher's Classroom! This is a specialized classroom designed to support a person with autism such as Christopher from the curious incident of the dog in the night-time written by Mark Haddon. This classroom is a concept made in Blender. Click on a different tab to read more.
